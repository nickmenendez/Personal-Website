Lifestyle

Life outside of work

Home base is Las Vegas, but when the snow starts falling I migrate to Lake Tahoe for the winter. Most of what I love doing happens outside — on a mountain, in the ocean, or somewhere down a dirt road.

Skiing

Palisades Tahoe is my home mountain, and winters revolve around it. The deeper obsession is backcountry skiing in the Sierra — skinning up far from the lifts and earning every turn down.

Surfing

I surf up and down the California coast, north and south. Once a year I pick somewhere new and chase waves there — Costa Rica, Nicaragua, El Salvador, and Baja Mexico so far.

Traveling

I spent 2025 traveling all around the world — and it only made the list of places I want to see longer. I try to get somewhere I've never been every year.

Coffee

Roasting and brewing started as a hobby and got out of hand — I'm now in the process of starting a small coffee roasting company.
